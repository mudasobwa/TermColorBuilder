256 Colors in Linux Terminal

Color Builder for Terminal

 

Nowadays terminals support 256 colors.

Why not use weird escape sentenses in your nifty logging? Choose your preferred colors and copy the crazyescapes below.

 

\e[m

MY_CLR="\e[m" \↵

Hello, world!

Escape sentences

Feel free to use one of the following escape sentenses (NB! Insert ^[ using Ctrl+V followed by Esc):

  • ^[[m
  • \e[m
  • \033[m

 

Background

Linux terminals have support for 256 colors. Not all of them know about it, though. To enable 256 colors simply export TERM=xterm256color and enjoy.
